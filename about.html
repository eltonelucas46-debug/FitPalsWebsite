Training Programmes

Dynamic Mobility

Definition

Dynamic Mobility Training is a form of movement training that improves joint mobility, flexibility, muscle activation, coordination, balance, and movement efficiency through controlled movements. Unlike static stretching, the body remains in motion throughout the exercises.

Objectives

Increase joint range of motion
Improve movement quality
Enhance athletic performance
Reduce injury risk
Prepare the body for exercise
Improve posture

Benefits

Better flexibility
Improved coordination
Increased blood circulation
Enhanced muscle activation
Faster reaction time
Reduced muscle stiffness
Improved balance
Better sports performance

Power
